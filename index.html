WHAT IS CROSSFIT?

CrossFit is a universal fitness program and conditioning system. It is used by many police, law-enforcement and military institutions as a functional strength and conditioning protocol. The program develops athletic skill and works on all fitness domains: endurance, stamina, strength, flexibility and coordination.

  • Professional athletes, preparing for competitions
  • Law-enforcement officers, preparing for active duty
  • Amateur athletes with mid to long experience in fitness
