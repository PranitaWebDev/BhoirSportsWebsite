BHOIR SPORTS COMPLEX

Welcome to Bhoir Sports Complex, a premium multi-sports and fitness facility designed for athletes of all levels. With a massive 20,000 sq. ft. training area, the complex offers a spacious and well-equipped environment for CrossFit, gymnastics and functional fitness activities.

The expansive hall provides enough room for both individual training and group sessions, ensuring comfort, safety, and freedom of movement. Our goal is to create a dynamic space where athletes can train, grow, and perform at their best.
