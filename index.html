About Us

Bhoir Sports Complex builds on the legacy of Bhoir Gymkhana, founded by Shri Mukund Bhojraj Bhoir and Smt. Vijaya Mukund Bhoir, which has been a cornerstone of sports excellence in Dombivli. With over 4,000 trained athletes, including 40+ international players, 150 national-level athletes and 250 state-level gymnasts and recipient of four "Shiv Chhatrapati Awards," Bhoir Gymkhana's proven success forms the foundation of Bhoir Sports Complex's vision for holistic athletic development. The complex also includes the Best Sporty Kids sports nursery program and partners with Dnyaneshwari Vidyalaya, a sports-based school, fostering early talent and providing comprehensive sports education.
